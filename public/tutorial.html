Bug Catch

Gameplay Tutorial

Welcome to "BugCatch"!

A fun and engaging web game where you aim to catch as many bugs as you can within the specified time to accumulate points and climb the leaderboard.

Introduce a "Login page"

Username: This is the primary field for users to input their unique username. Since the username is the main identifier for accumulating scores

Avatar Profile: This field allows users to select an avatar profile image.

Introduce a "Gameplay"

Gameplay: The primary objective is to catch as many bugs as possible within the specified time. Use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with the game.

Score Tracking: As you catch bugs, your score will increase. Keep an eye on your score as you aim to reach new high scores and compete with other players.

Introduce a "Leaderboard"

Player Rankings: View the rankings of other players based on their scores.

Your Position: See where you stand in the rankings compared to other players.

Motivation: Use the leaderboard as motivation to improve your score and climb the ranks.
